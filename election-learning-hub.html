India's Lok Sabha General Election
Every stage we've studied, mapped to how it actually plays out in the world's largest democracy.
Context: India uses First-Past-the-Post across 543 constituencies. Each constituency elects one Member of Parliament (MP). The party or alliance winning 272+ seats forms the government. Elections are held every 5 years under the supervision of the independent Election Commission of India.
